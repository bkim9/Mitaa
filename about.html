About

Welcome to Psychiatry & Neurology Medical Group of Los Angeles
We do advanced diagnostic testing for neurological disorders

Our Mission

At JKC Clinic, our mission is to provide compassionate, comprehensive, and cutting-edge neurological care with a specialized focus on traumatic brain injuries, cognitive impairment, headaches, epilepsy, and psychiatry. We are committed to enhancing the quality of life for our patients by delivering personalized treatment plans that integrate the latest advancements in medical science, technology, and holistic well-being.

Our Team

Our team of experienced medical professionals includes board-certified neurologist, Dr. Kyung Cho and his staff who are all committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. We work together to ensure that each patient receives the individualized attention they need.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services, including general neurology office visits, neurofunctional testing, ANS (autonomic nervous system) testing, EEGs (electroencephalogram), TMS (transcranial magnetic stimulation) treatment, Alzheimer Detection blood tests, and other procedures in our office. Our goal is to provide comprehensive care that addresses all of our patients' health needs.

Insurance & New Patient Information

Welcome to Neurology Clinic of Jacksonville. We accept Medicare, Blue Cross Anthem, Blue Shield of California, Aetna, United Health Care, Cigna and most other commercial plans. Please contact us to verify coverage of your plan. We ask that you please: Arrive 15-to-20 minutes before your appointment. Bring your insurance cards and picture ID/Driver’s License. Bring your list of all current medications or all medication bottles. Bring any previous neurologic testing results (MRI, CT scan, EEG, EMG/Nerve conduction study, etc.)
