Kyung K. Cho, MD

Diplomate, American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology

Neurologist, Memory Clinic Program

After graduated from Seoul National University Medical School in 1978, Dr. Cho has been a distinguished and highly respected figure in the field of neurology, with over 35 years of unwavering commitment to providing exceptional patient care and advancing the understanding and treatment of neurological conditions. As a board-certified neurologist and double-board certified epilepsy specialist, Dr. Cho's expertise spans a wide spectrum, reflecting his unparalleled dedication to improving the lives of those he serves. For more than three decades, Dr. Cho has been a beacon of excellence in the medical community. His own private practice has been a haven for patients seeking comprehensive and personalized neurological care. Through his meticulous diagnostic approach and tailored treatment plans, he has earned a reputation for his compassionate bedside manner and unparalleled expertise. Dr. Cho's influence extends beyond his private practice, as he is currently affiliated with the esteemed Cedars-Sinai Medical Center. This affiliation underscores his commitment to staying at the forefront of medical advancements and collaborating with other top professionals in the field. Dr. Cho's dedication to education and scholarly pursuits has left an indelible mark on both the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) and the University of Southern California (USC). He served as a former Clinical Associate Professor of Neurology at UCLA, where he imparted his knowledge and passion to aspiring medical professionals. In recognition of his profound contributions to the field, he was also a valued board member of USC's Geriatric Medicine program, where he contributed his expertise to addressing the unique neurological needs of older adults.
