Kyung K. Cho, MD

Diplomate, American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology

Neurologist, Memory Clinic Program

Dr. Cho is a distinguished and highly respected figure in the field of neurology, with over 35 years of unwavering commitment to providing exceptional patient care and advancing the understanding and treatment of neurological conditions. As a board-certified neurologist and double-board certified epilepsy specialist, Dr. Cho's expertise spans a wide spectrum, reflecting his unparalleled dedication to improving the lives of those he serves. Dr. Kyung Cho, MD is a neurology specialist in Los Angeles, CA and has over 44 years of experience in the medical field. He graduated from SEOUL NATIONAL UNIVERSITY / COLLEGE OF MEDICINE in 1978.
